About

The mission of Conquest Capital Ventures is to establish and maintain an undergraduate venture capital fund at the Marshall School of Business that invests in socially conscious, local start -ups. In addition to providing seed capital for social ventures, the fund will create a unique experience for students to develop a deeper understanding of start-ups, social enterprises and financial sponsorship.
